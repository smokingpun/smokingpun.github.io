Xoomworks – tech company

Xoomworks in an Artios client who we helped with a major website redesign. A technology company with a fantastic client list, they specialises in procurement, business intelligence and technology outsourcing. Pivoting from their original model of one website, we helped them develop a major web presence encompassing three microsites.

The challenge was to ensure consistency across the microsites while still communicating the key messages of each specialism. By approaching this project content-first, we were able to concentrate on developing an online presence that specifically targeted the needs of Xoomworks' clients.

bcalm – medical product company

Bcalm is an Artios client that we helped build an online presence for the launch of their new product, an innovative medical device that quickly provides relief for people during panic attacks.

The challenge was to ensure that we developed a brand that appeals to people in an emotionally-charged condition, something which is often done very badly (as anyone who has browsed panic-related sites will know!). Early-stage user research was critical in helping us build a brand that was calming, safe, accessible, and scientific.

Vimcasts – tech startup

Vimcasts wanted to increase people's engagement with the site by making the content easier to navigate on all devices, increase traffic to the site through a broader social media strategy, and give more visibility to training, publications and pro-screencasts.

I helped with user research and interpretation of the metrics. I also wireframed key pages (for different viewports), including two new landing page, as well as edited existing copy and created new copy where necessary. I've blogged about my content strategy work on Vimcasts.
