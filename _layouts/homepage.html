What is Content Strategy?

You're probably here because you've heard people talking about content strategy. A lot of people. And it sounds like a great idea. Who doesn't want content that is user-focused, meaninful and future-friendly? And your own content – well, it's not quite there yet. No problem. You've come to the right place. My name is Hannah Adcock Neil and I'm here to help you do less, better. Here's what I can do:

Analysis and Auditing

You'll tell me what your key business objectives are. I'll analyse your content to see whether it's supporting these objectives and is user-friendly. I'll also do a content audit of your existing material – or part of it at least – to see what your content looks like at the moment.

Analytics

This is where I use Google Analytics, KISSmetrics, Crazy Egg, Topsy – basically, whatever's most relevant and fits your budget – to see what your users are doing. I'll then have a think about why. If you're feeling generous, then we could also do some user-testing at this point.

Strategy

I'll go over the results of my data crunching and anaylsis with you. Then we'll sit down over a cup of tea and develop a plan of action. I'll bring some sugestions to the table – and maybe some cake. You will too. Your content strategy has to be sustainable over the longer-term, which means working out who on your team will need to do what. And whether this is possible in the real world rather than a fantastical universe where everyone is super efficient all of the time. And technology never lets you down. Not ever.

Implementation

You'll start implementing your new content strategy. I'll be there to help with training and questions. Then we'll have a sign off meeting and agree how we'll measure the success of your new content strategy. Congratulations! You now have content that is working for its keep.
